EGG THOKKU...

                    

Egg thokku is an easy and quick side dish that serves with chapati and rice. It tastes spicy and flavorful with spices, tomatoes, and tamarind.Egg thokku is a delicious South Indian recipe with spices, tomatoes, and tamarind.

PROCEDURE

  1. In a saucepan place eggs in a single layer, add water to cover the eggs, add a pinch of salt and bring to a vigorous boil. Turn off the heat and cover the pan with a lid.

  2. Allow the eggs to sit in the hot water for 15 minutes. Drain the water from eggs completely, crack open the eggs and serve.

  3. Cook tomatoes thoroughly until soft and mushy. If the tomatoes remain uncooked then the gravy will taste raw tomato flavor.
